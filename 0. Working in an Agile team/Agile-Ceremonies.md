# Communication and Alignment
- Agile ceremonies help with team communication and alignment by ensuring everyone is aware of what they need to do, and how others in the team are tracking. By consistently checking in and revising tasks, potential bottlenecks or issues can be quickly addressed and solved, or excess work redelegated across the team
- I believe the most important ceremony for my role will be the daily standups. Due to being an intern, I expect to encounter a lot of new and unfamilar concepts and tasks throughout my role. Thus, I believe having regular checkins with my supervisor and fellow team members will be crucial to helping solve any issues I encounter or questions I might have
