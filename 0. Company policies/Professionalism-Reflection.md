# Research
- Example of professional behaviour would include approaching team meetings prepared, utilising the appropriate channels, and using polite and proactivite communication
- Unprofessional behaviour may include multitasking during meetings, not taking notes, not using the correct channels, or using rude or inappropriate communication
- Respectful communication in remote teams means using a friendly tone with clear intentions and ensuring prompt responses. Disagreements should be handled professionally and critisism should not be taken personally.
- Feedback should be accepted willingly and points raised should be addressed and actioned. If giving feedback, critisism should be on the work done and not directed at the person.
